Our mission:

Our story

The founders were united by their constant love for finding, brewing and sharing excellent coffee experiences, which brought them together. 
The idea that Andreea, Alex and Jessica had in common was what gave rise to Café Serenitea.  

Quality commitment

Fundamentally, we're dedicated to finding the best beans, using artisan roasting techniques, and constantly producing cups of perfection. From the farms where our beans are obtained to the expert workmanship in our brewing process, we are committed to excellence.

Community connection

In addition to providing excellent coffee, we take pleasure in creating a friendly neighbourhood. We're more than simply a café; we're a gathering place where people come to make friends, discuss ideas, and cherish special moments.
Our goal is to create a space that is comfortable and familiar yet feels like a home away from home.
